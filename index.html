Why the Games Industry?

Games like Resident Evil, Half-Life, and Silent Hill showed me early on what this medium is capable of. Not just of producing mindless entertainment, but genuine atmosphere and tension. World building that will stay with you. Having spent time as a teenager attempting to build something in that vein taught me that the gap between playing a great game and between making one is enormous.

That gap is what I find interesting. It is the technical depth behind a convincing online game world that immerses a prospective player. It's the persistent systems, the network architecture, the security, the and responsive design. Those areas are exactly the kind of problem space that I want to work in. I have the foundation to contribute meaningfully as well as the motivation to keep pushing further into it.
